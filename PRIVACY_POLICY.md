# Privacy Policy | 隐私政策

**Last Updated | 最后更新:** 2026-03-05

## English

### Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data.**

ChatHop (聊天兔子) extension:
- Does NOT collect personal information
- Does NOT track your browsing activity
- Does NOT send data to external servers
- Does NOT use cookies or analytics
- Does NOT require any account or registration

### How It Works

The extension operates entirely within your browser:
1. It only activates on qianwen.com and tongyi.aliyun.com pages
2. It reads page content locally to generate the timeline
3. All processing happens on your device
4. No data ever leaves your browser

### Permissions

The extension requests minimal permissions:
- **activeTab**: To access the current tab content
- **storage**: To save your preferences (sidebar state)

### Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with any third-party services.

### Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here.

### Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/openclaw/qianwen-timeline).

## 中文

### 数据收集

**我们不收集、存储或传输任何用户数据。**

本扩展：
- 不收集个人信息
- 不追踪您的浏览活动
- 不向外部服务器发送数据
- 不使用 Cookie 或分析工具

### 工作原理

扩展完全在您的浏览器中运行：
1. 仅在 qianwen.com 和 tongyi.aliyun.com 页面激活

3. 所有处理都在您的设备上进行

本扩展不集成任何第三方服务。
